AN ODE TO SUMMER

An Ode to Summer is a short 8 level demo of a musical puzzle game that challenges players to create a tune by chaining notes together. The game is designed to be fun and engaging while also teaching players about basic music theory. Made using Unity, it implements a real-time lighting system is a 2d environment, mimicing 3d lighting conditions and enchancing the atmosphere.

PROJECT HELIOS

Project "Helios" is a gravity simulation using Newton's universal law of gravitation and Kepler's 1st law of planetary motion to simulate the movements of objects in space under the influence of gravity. This simulation is used to model the behavior of celestial bodies, such as planets, moons, satellites, and comets.

MY ANIMANGA

As an anime and manga lover, it can be difficult to keep track of all the different series you are watching or reading, especially if you are following multiple series at once. This is where a list application can come in handy.

Built using HTML, CSS and Electron JS, this tracker allows users to keep track of the series they are watching or reading, including the episode or chapter they are on, their progress through the series, and any ratings they wish to add. Users could also search for new series to add to their list and view information about each series.

CHECK IT OUT

PROJECT POLITISMOS

Project "Politismos" is a trading game demo that allows players to trade goods across procedurally generated islands. Importantly, the game world is generated procedurally, meaning that the landscape and islands are randomly generated by an algorithm rather than being pre-designed by developers.

The player must carefully manage their inventory and finances to maximize their profits. As the player becomes more successful, they can invest in their trading company, expanding their fleet of traders across the island.

CHECK IT OUT

WATCH MY NUCLEUS

Watch my Nucleus is a game made for the Ludum Dare 49 Game Jam, with the theme "Unstable". The game revolves around the concept of an unstable radioactive atom that is on the verge of collapsing, and the player's goal is to prevent it from doing so.

The game was well received and won 61st out of 2939 participants in the "Theme" category. It's unique concept and fast-paced gameplay made it a popular choice among participants and judges alike.
